About Us




Brewing Connections Since Day One


What began as a single store with a passion for exceptional coffee has grown into one of the most recognized and beloved coffeehouses in the world. At Starbucks, we’ve always believed that coffee is more than just a beverage — it’s a bridge between people, cultures, and ideas. Every bean we roast, every cup we serve, and every smile we share is part of our commitment to making a positive difference in the communities we serve.



Our Mission


To inspire and nurture the human spirit — one person, one cup, and one neighborhood at a time. We strive to create a warm and welcoming environment where everyone feels valued and connected.



Our Values


At Starbucks, we are guided by our core values: integrity, respect, diversity, and excellence. We believe in treating each other with dignity and respect, embracing diversity in all its forms, and striving for excellence in everything we do.



Join Us


Whether you are a coffee lover, a community advocate, or simply curious about what we do, we invite you to join us on this journey. Explore our products, visit our stores, and become part of the Starbucks family.
